About Me

I'm a multimedia reporter/ storyteller at the University of Missouri. I believe that everyone has a story to tell that is shaped by their culture and experience. I would like to use my languages and multicultural background to tell those stories that connect audiences and subjects. I'm at the beginning of my career and I'm looking for opportunities that will help me grow my reporting skills.
